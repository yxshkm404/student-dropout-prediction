    🎓 Welcome to the Student Dropout Prediction System¶

    An intelligent, early-warning system that helps teachers and principals safeguard the future of their students.

    ❓ The Problem¶

    Every year, countless students drop out of school because they struggle in silence. By the time a teacher or principal realizes a student is severely failing, it is often too late to help them.

    Students don't just fail overnight; there is usually a pattern of declining grades across different subjects that goes unnoticed in the busy day-to-day life of a school.

    💡 The Solution¶

    What if a computer could look at a student's test scores, find hidden patterns, and alert the teacher before the student fails?

    This Student Dropout Prediction System does exactly that. It acts as an artificial intelligence (AI) assistant for educators.

    By simply inputting a student's recent test scores, the system uses Machine Learning to: 1. Predict if the student is at risk of dropping out. 2. Explain why they are at risk (e.g., pointing out exactly which subjects are causing trouble). 3. Group students by the severity of their risk, so teachers know who needs urgent help today versus who needs mild tutoring next week.

    👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Who Is This For?¶

    This system is built with three types of users in mind:

    • 👨‍🏫 Teachers: Can add their students, input test scores, run the AI predictions, and get actionable advice to help struggling learners.
    • 👩‍💼 Principals: Can oversee the entire school, approve teacher accounts, and view beautiful, school-wide statistics to see overall passing/risk trends.
    • 🎒 Students: Can log into their own portal to track their grades and see their own AI prediction results, keeping them motivated and informed.
